About

A free, peer-reviewed journal of the health sciences.

The journal publishes original research, reviews, case reports, and scholarly correspondence across the health sciences. There are no fees to publish or to read, and submissions are welcomed from researchers everywhere. Every research article is assessed by independent reviewers under a double-blind process.

Scope & sections

Original Research

Empirical studies across the health sciences — observational, clinical, and laboratory.

Review Articles

Narrative reviews, systematic reviews, and meta-analyses synthesising current evidence.

Case Reports

Instructive clinical cases — rare presentations and novel diagnostic or therapeutic approaches.

Short Communications

Concise reports of preliminary findings and focused observations meriting timely release.

Editorials

Commissioned perspectives on topics of contemporary relevance in the health sciences.

Letters to the Editor

Scholarly correspondence on published articles and matters of relevance to readers.

For researchers

Built for the people doing the research.

Postgraduate residents, clinicians, and faculty can turn dissertations, clinical audits, and field studies into citable, openly accessible publications — without paying a fee.

How publishing works

From submission to open access.

STEP 01

Submit online

Upload your manuscript through the journal portal with guided formatting and declarations.

STEP 02

Editorial check

Scope, ethics, and reporting standards are screened early to avoid slow, avoidable rejections.

STEP 03

Peer review

Double-blind review by independent experts, with transparent decision tracking.

STEP 04

Open access

Accepted articles are published open access — readable by anyone, with no paywall.
