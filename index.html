How publishing works

From draft to publication in weeks, not months

A guided, fully online workflow modelled on fast open-access publishing — with editor-supported guidance on formatting and reporting instead of paid author-side services.

STEP 01

Submit online

Structured templates for every article type. Upload once — the platform guides formatting, declarations and metadata.

STEP 02

Editorial pre-check

Scope, ethics, plagiarism, reporting checklists and journal fit are screened early to prevent slow, avoidable rejections.

STEP 03

Peer review clock

Single-blind specialty review with transparent tracking, reviewer reminders and article-level decision history.

STEP 04

Free open access

HTML, PDF, structured metadata and public discoverability — readable by anyone without a paywall.

Community Impact Score · planned

Peer review does not stop at publication

A planned feature for a future release: each article will be able to carry a post-publication Community Impact Score — a 0–10 rating contributed by registered clinicians and researchers, with specialist ratings weighted higher and transparent rater counts.

  • Weighted by expertise. Specialty reviewers contribute more weight to papers in their own domain.
  • Article-level assessment. Readers evaluate the paper, not just the journal brand.
  • Transparent counts. Every score shows number of raters and broad specialty composition.
